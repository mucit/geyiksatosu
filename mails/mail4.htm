I know someone personally around here, someone very strange in many ways:

1. He is not native to this country (professingly of Denmark origin), but he speaks my mother tongue very efficiently and fluently without even a single pronunciation error, (well, actually he misstresses one word, but many of my fellow countrymen mispronounce it as well) although it is radically different from Germanic, Anglo-Saxon and Latin languages.

It is amazing: Word plays, new words and new structures but all the way so natural that even though I do know the writer, I can not tell apart. He has published several books, in each of which he has something to say about virtually everything: Mainly physics and cosmology, chemistry, biology, math, economics, politics and so on. He is a journalist, astrologer, tv script writer...

3. He used to have no id until 5 months ago. (He says he was born in 1945 or 47, I forgot the exact year).

7. He seriously claims to have developed some physical theories, for instance on the white holes and the wormholes, but the international science mafia, as he calls it, overlooked him and did not award him the Nobel Prize he deserved.

8. He says he was working at Nasa as a technician when Apollo-14 landed on the moon, and saw, with about 300 other technicians, the ufos escorting the spacecraft. 9.
